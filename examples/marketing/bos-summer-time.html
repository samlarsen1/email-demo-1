If you’re looking to open a personal pension, we have two options for you to choose from:
Managed for you
Choose a Ready-Made Pension and our experts will fully manage your investments for you, based on your chosen retirement age.
 
Managed by you
Choose a Self-Invested Personal Pension (SIPP) and decide where to invest yourself. If you’re a director of a limited company, you can also make company contributions.

Don't fall for a scam
Remember, we'll never call to tell you to move money to another account or to ask for your full banking details. Unless you called for help, avoid any download that lets someone take control of your device. If you get a call like this, hang up. A genuine email from us will always include your title, surname and part of your account number or postcode. We’ll never send an email with a link for you to log on or give personal and banking details. If you get a message like this, don’t reply and delete it.
What to expect from our emails

We will:
• Always include your name and part of your postcode.
• Sometimes include links to more information to help you.
• Sometimes send you links to our secure app, to take you straight to the information you need.

We will never:
• Send you to a web page that asks for your log in, personal or banking details.

If you're not sure that an email is from us:
• Forward it to security@bankofscotland.co.uk and then delete it.
• Speak to us to check. You could do this by messaging us from your app or Online banking.

We have lots of information to keep you and your money safe on our website. Just visit our Fraud Hub.
Calls and online sessions may be monitored and recorded. Not all Telephone Banking services are available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
Online fraud guarantee
We do all we can to protect you online. Even in the unlikely event of fraud, we promise you won't lose out. As an online share dealing customer, you automatically benefit from our online fraud guarantee:
If you use our online service and become a victim of fraud, we guarantee you won't lose any money from your account, and will always be reimbursed in full.

For the vast majority of our customers, this situation will never arise, especially if you take a few simple steps:
• Keep your password secure and do not let anyone else make use of your security details, even if they share a joint account with you
• Do not let anyone watch you enter your security details and log off after each online session
• Carry out regular virus checks on your devices and have the latest operating system and web browser installed

If you've been grossly negligent, we will not refund any money taken from your account before you have told us your security details have been lost, stolen or could be misused. We won't give you a refund if you've acted fraudulently. Find out more about how to protect yourself online.
