The joy of progress
Feel a mini burst of joy when you achieve something? That’s a dopamine hit.

There’s something rewarding about seeing your efforts pay off – even in small ways. Building a savings habit can help build what we call your ‘Money-mentum’, and give you that feel-good feeling.
So, how can you keep that 'Money-mentum' going?
Set up a savings goal in the app – even if it's a small one.

Giving your goal a name makes it personal. Linking it to your savings account helps you stay on track. And watching your progress build over time? That can really keep you motivated.

Think of it as a practical step toward something important – a family trip, home improvement, or simply peace of mind. Smaller goals are easier to manage, and each milestone brings a real sense of accomplishment.
