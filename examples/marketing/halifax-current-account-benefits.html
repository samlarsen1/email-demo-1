Cashback Extras
To be eligible for Cashback Extras you need to be over 18, have a Halifax debit and/or credit card and be registered for Online Banking.

Up to 15% Cashback - activate your offers using our Mobile Banking app or Online Banking and earn as you shop with Cashback Extras. Retailer offers and cashback amounts vary.

We do everything we can to make sure offer details are accurate, but we reserve the right to withdraw or correct any offers. This won’t affect offers you’ve already activated.

Any cashback earned on credit card purchases will be paid to the primary cardholder's selected account.
You’ll need to stay registered for Cashback Extras and have a Halifax credit card account or an active Halifax debit card to receive your cashback.
