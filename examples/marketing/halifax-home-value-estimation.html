Security information
It can be tricky to spot a scam message. We’ll never send you an email with a direct link to a page that wants you to sign in, or give personal or banking details.

If we send you an email, it will always include your title, surname and part of your postcode.

If you're not sure that an email is from us:

• Forward it to security@halifax.co.uk and then delete it.
• Or call us on 0345 602 0000 (+44 113 279 8302 from outside the UK). Lines are open 8am - 8pm, seven days a week.
